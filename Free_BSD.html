Status

The FreeBSD/ppc port is still a Tier 2 platform. That means it is not being fully support by our security officer, release engineers and toolchain maintainers.

Latest News

  • 03 April, 2009: Support for the PowerPC G5 has been committed to SVN
  • 03 March, 2008: Support for the Freescale ® PowerQUICC III MPC85XX family system-in-chip development boards has been committed into CVS.
  • 27 February, 2008: FreeBSC 7.0 is the first release to officially support the FreeBSD/ppc port.
